Rule the pipeline enforces: only sources marked CC0 or CC-BY are allowed, and every clip's source URL + license is logged. If a claim is ever filed, you can prove clearance in one click. Never pull from "free stock" sites that resell licensed footage — that's where claims come from.

How to verify a clip is safe

  • Open the clip's license page. Look for the literal words CC0 or CC-BY, or a platform license that says "free for commercial use, no attribution required."
  • For CC-BY, copy the credit line into your video description: Footage: [Title] by [Author] via [Source], CC BY [version].
  • Log it: visuals_pro records source_url + license per clip.
  • If a source is ambiguous, skip it. There is always CC0 alternatives for any topic.
